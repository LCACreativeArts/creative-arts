Welcome to Le Comte-Andrews, Creative Arts!
It is on this site that I will post my many stories for all to enjoy. For nearly a decade I have been writing as a hobby, and it brings me so much joy. Though I have gotten much better at this art form over the years, the time in which I will be a published author is still far away.
So, instead of letting these tales rot away in my folders, I shall let them see some light here.
Some of these stories are broken up into Universes, Series, and Parts. They are listed chronologically, and it is recommended that you follow this order.
That being said feel free to browse them in any order you like. One of my goals is to write stories that are enjoyable on their own, so you'll probably be fine.
Thank you for spending your time here, and experiencing the worlds I am so often lost within.
